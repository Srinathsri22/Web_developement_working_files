Hot Potato Salad

Ingredients Method

Ingredients

  • 2 boiled Potatoes
  • few corriander leaves
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp Tomato ketchup
  • Oil for deep frying

Method

  1. Slice the potatoes as slim as possible and boil them
  2. Deep fry the boiled potatoes and transfer it to the china cup
  3. Add the necessary spices like chilly powder with butter
  4. Add few corriander leaves as toppings
  5. Serve it with tomate ketchup.
